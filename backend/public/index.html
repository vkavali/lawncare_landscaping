What it can't do

Satellite photos are old. Sometimes your customer's house isn't in them.

Every free basemap is a photograph from a past flyover — not a live picture. In a brand-new subdivision the plane flew when the lot was still dirt. Cuadrilla gives you four imagery sources because they fly on different dates, plus a hand-measure mode for when none of them show the house. We would rather tell you that than pretend.
